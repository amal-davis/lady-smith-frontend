About this property

Beachfront and Leisure: OBLU XPERIENCE Ailafushi in Male offers a beachfront setting with an infinity swimming pool, spa facilities, sauna, fitness centre, sun terrace, and free WiFi.

Comfortable Accommodations: Rooms feature private bathrooms, bathrobes, tea and coffee makers, and more.

Dining Experience: The modern, family-friendly restaurant serves seafood, local, and international cuisines with halal, vegetarian, and gluten-free options.
