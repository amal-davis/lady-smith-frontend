About Us

ABOUT US

THE LADY SMITH RESORTS

With its humble beginnings as a bus operator, Lady Smith Resort of Companies has expanded far and wide with multiple resorts all over Kerala. The company aims to provide a place for visitors to enjoy their stay in the indigenous surroundings to embrace nature in its rustic form. The properties are set in serene environments preserving the ecology of the land and leaving the natural beauty of the flora and fauna intact. The entire setting of the resorts are meant to evoke an earthy feeling of love of nature, where visitors can enjoy living in the warm, caressing lap of nature while enjoying the comforts of modern amenities.

HOTELS & RESORTS

Lady Smith Resort has always upheld its values of humanity and protecting nature while providing maximum value to its customers. The focus is on delivering beautiful experiences and activities to the customers, so when they go back home, they go back with refreshing memories of the place.

The group has been making one punctilious leap after another, with each of these resorts located at the different key locations in Kerala...

A JOURNEY THROUGH NATURE

At each of these locations, you can hear, feel and experience the true and raw beauty of nature whispering mystical stories. You can tune out all the hustle and bustle of a busy life and easily embrace these stories like the most precious stories you've ever collected.
